❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a profile?

Visit the Profile page, fill in your details, and click Save. You'll be ready to start browsing or posting jobs!

How are payments handled?

All payments are processed securely on our platform. Payment is held until the job is completed, protecting both workers and customers.

How long does support take?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days (Mon-Fri, 9am-6pm UTC).

Can I edit my posted jobs?

Yes! You can edit or delete jobs you've posted from your profile dashboard at any time before a worker applies.

Is my personal information safe?

Absolutely. We use encryption and follow industry best practices to keep your data safe and secure.

What if I have a dispute?

We have a dispute resolution process. Contact us with details and we'll help mediate and resolve the issue.
